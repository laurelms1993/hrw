Who We Are:

We are the women of the Kappa Nu chapter of Alpha Chi Omega at Carnegie Mellon University. We are Computer Scientists, Mathematicians, Dramaturgs, Historians, Creative Writers, Engineers, Artists, Physicists, Architects, Biologists, Statiticians, Writers, Singers, Business Women, Philosophers, Chemists and more.

We strive to make a difference on our campus by empowering women and promoting domestic violence awareness.

Visit our chapter website at cmualphachiomega.com and our facebook page at CMU Alpha Chi Omega.
