Alpha Chi Omega supports Healthy Relationships Week

Who We Are:

We are the women of the Kappa Nu chapter of Alpha Chi Omega at Carnegie Mellon University. We are computer Scientists, Mathematicians, Dramaturgs, Historians, Creative Writers, Engineers, Artists, Physicists, Architects, Biologists, Stateticians, Writers, Singers, Business Women, Philosophers, Chemists, and more.
We are a relatively new chapter and we strive to make a difference on our campus by empowering women and promoting domestic violence awareness.
